<h3>Instructions for Copying Interactive Team Space Documents to Your Personal Confluence Space</h3><p>It&rsquo;s a good idea to have copies of interactive documents, such as checklists, wikis and forms, in your personal space. Follow these steps to copy them:</p><h3>Step 1: Copy the Document</h3><ol start="1"><li><p>Open the document by clicking on its title.</p></li><li><p>Click on the <strong>More actions</strong> (&bull;&bull;&bull;) menu at the top right corner of the page.</p></li><li><p>Select <strong>Copy</strong> from the dropdown menu.</p></li></ol><h3>Step 2: Choose the Destination</h3><ol start="1"><li><p>In the <strong>Copy page</strong> dialog, select <strong>Choose a Space</strong>.</p></li><li><p>From the list of available spaces, choose your personal space. If you don&rsquo;t see it, type your name to search for it.</p></li><li><p>Update the <strong>Title</strong> if you want to give the document a new name.</p></li><li><p>Click <strong>Copy</strong> to create a copy of the document in your personal space.</p></li></ol><h3>Step 3: Verify the Copy</h3><ol start="1"><li><p>Navigate to your personal space by clicking on your profile picture at the top right corner and selecting <strong>Your Personal Space</strong>.</p></li><li><p>Verify that the document has been copied correctly by locating it in your personal space and opening it.</p></li></ol><h3>Additional Tips</h3><ul><li><p><strong>Organize Your Space</strong>: Create folders or pages to organize your documents logically within your personal space.</p></li><li><p><strong>Keep Updated</strong>: Regularly check for updates in the shared spaces and copy the latest versions to your personal space if necessary.</p></li></ul>
